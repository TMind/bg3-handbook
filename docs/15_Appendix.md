## 💡 Practical Principles

**Markers:** ⭐⭐⭐

- Inspect enemies before important actions.
- Read initiative and control dangerous enemies early.
- Do not waste rare alchemy ingredients in the Any slot.
- Recheck vendors after Long Rests.
- Do not hoard consumables forever when they can make a difficult fight much easier.

## 📚 Glossary: Spells, Items, and Mechanics ^glossary

**Markers:** ⭐⭐ ⏱️

This glossary explains terms that are referenced elsewhere in the handbook. It is not a complete BG3 encyclopedia; it only covers terms that matter for the practical tips in this vault.

### ✨ Spells, Buffs, and Magical Effects ^glossary-spells

| Term | Type | Practical meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Longstrider | Spell / buff | Until Long Rest movement buff; available to Bard, Druid, Ranger, Wizard, Eldritch Knight, Arcane Trickster, or Ritual Caster. See [Preparation and Battle Setup](09_Preparation_and_Battle_Setup.md). |
| Mage Armor | Spell / buff | Sorcerer/Wizard-style defensive buff; raises AC for unarmored characters and some summons. |
| Aid | Spell / buff | Cleric/Paladin buff that raises maximum HP for the party; strong before difficult areas. |
| Protection from Poison | Spell / buff | Cleric/Druid/Ranger/Paladin buff; useful before poison-heavy areas or enemies. |
| Warding Bond | Spell / buff | Cleric buff; advanced defensive setup that improves AC/saves and splits damage with the caster. |
| Death Ward | Spell / buff | Cleric buff, or Bard through Magical Secrets; protects a key character from one lethal drop. |
| Freedom of Movement | Spell / buff | Bard/Cleric/Druid buff; helps against movement and control problems. |
| Heroes' Feast | Spell / buff | Late-game Cleric/Druid party durability and protection buff. |
| Goodberry | Spell / consumable-like buff | Druid/Ranger spell that creates small healing berries; can trigger gear that reacts to healing. |
| See Invisibility | Spell / scroll effect | Detects invisible enemies; scroll use preserves the elixir slot. |
| Light | Cantrip / utility | Can be cast on a cheap object, then moved or thrown to create a portable light source. |
| Find Familiar | Spell / summon | Scout, lure, small-route helper, or pre-rest summon that can persist after resting. |
| Mage Hand | Spell / summon-like utility | Moves, throws, or shoves objects from risky positions without exposing a party member. |
| Silence | Spell / control utility | Stops many spellcasting options, mutes noisy actions, and protects creatures inside from Thunder damage. |
| Web | Spell / surface control | Restrains and slows enemies; can also act as a soft landing surface when Feather Fall is unavailable. |
| Darkness / Fog Cloud | Spell / obscuring control | Blocks sight lines and creates heavily obscured space for repositioning or stealth actions. |
| Create or Destroy Water | Spell / surface utility | Extinguishes flames, creates water for lightning or ice follow-ups, and can reveal likely invisible targets. |
| Sanctuary | Spell / defensive utility | Bonus-action protection for a rescued ally or support caster who is not directly harming enemies. |
| Grease | Spell / surface control | Creates difficult flammable terrain; can funnel enemies or extend an existing fire surface. |
| Ray of Frost | Spell / cantrip | Slows targets and can put out fire while creating water or ice follow-up opportunities. |
| Bless | Spell / buff | Improves reliability; can also come from [[06_Elixirs_and_Potions#^elixir-of-heroism\|Elixir of Heroism]]. |
| Enhance Ability | Spell / skill support | Improves important ability checks. See [Magic and Useful Items](08_Magic_and_Useful_Items.md). |
| Bardic Inspiration | Class feature / skill support | Adds reliability to important checks and other key rolls. |
| Speak with Dead | Spell / utility | One poor target does not waste the spell for the day; try other relevant bodies. |
| Feather Fall | Spell / potion effect | Makes vertical exploration and large drops safer. |
| Misty Step | Spell | Short teleport for positioning, exploration, or route access. |
| Knock | Spell | Opens locks when lockpicking is unavailable or inconvenient. |
| Enlarge/Reduce | Spell | Size utility for routing, small openings, and some Strength plans. |
| Gaseous Form | Spell / potion effect | Bypasses awkward spaces, bars, and body-blocking problems. |
| Remove Curse | Spell | Cleanup option for cursed characters or items. |
| Magic Missile | Spell | Multi-hit spell; useful when several visible objects or levers must be triggered. |
| Healing Word | Spell | Ranged bonus-action pickup for downed allies; often better than Help. |
| Darkvision | Vision effect | Helps characters without natural darkvision in dark areas; can come from [[06_Elixirs_and_Potions#^elixir-of-darkvision\|Elixir of Darkvision]]. |
| Disguise Self / Seeming / Wild Shape | Form or appearance tools | Can matter when dialogue, identity, or Speak with Dead interactions depend on appearance or form. |
| Mask of the Shapeshifter | Form-change item | Can enable disguise, size, and race-conditional equipment checks; re-equip gear if a conditional bonus does not appear after changing form. |
| Transmuter Stone | Class feature / buff item | Transmutation Wizard feature; one stone can be passed to another character and movement is often the easiest default value. |
| Weapon Bond | Eldritch Knight feature | Eldritch Knight Fighter feature; can protect a valuable weapon from disarm effects and support thrown-weapon setups. |

### 🧰 Items and Item Buffs ^glossary-items

| Term | Type | Practical meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Broodmother's Revenge | Item / weapon buff trigger | Adds poison damage after the wearer is healed; can be triggered with small heals such as Goodberry. |
| Drakethroat Glaive | Item / weapon buff | Can add elemental damage to another weapon; useful as a pre-fight item buff. |
| Flail of Ages | Item / weapon buff | Can add elemental weapon value but does not stack with every other weapon buff. |
| Scroll of See Invisibility | Scroll | Long-duration detection option that preserves the elixir slot. |
| Stat stick | Equipment concept | Gear worn for passive bonuses even when the character does not mainly attack with that item. |
| Bagged explosives | Inventory / environment setup | Container-based explosive setup; powerful but consumes resources and requires strict ally spacing. |

### ⚔️ Combat and Build Terms ^glossary-combat

| Term | Type | Practical meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Initiative | Combat order | Determines who acts first; use it to choose targets before enemies act. |
| Advantage / Disadvantage | Roll modifier | Advantage improves reliability; disadvantage can waste strong attacks. |
| Concentration | Spell constraint | Only one concentration effect can stay active per caster; damage can break it. |
| Manual concentration cancel | UI action | End an active concentration spell from the character portrait when it would hurt allies, block movement, or be replaced by a better spell. |
| Opportunity Attack | Reaction | Melee enemies may punish movement away from them. |
| Disengage | Action | Prevents opportunity attacks when leaving melee. |
| Jump | Movement / bonus action | Often beats walking around hazards or blocked routes; improved by Strength. |
| Throw | Action | Moves objects, bombs, potions, or enemies when Strength and positioning allow it; weapons need the Thrown tag to use their real weapon damage. |
| Shove | Bonus action | Moves enemies off ledges, out of chokepoints, or into hazards; can also move or wake allies when the direction is safe. |
| Improvised Weapon | Action / combat option | Uses a nearby object or creature as a weapon; can provide easy bludgeoning damage or reposition heavy objects. |
| Dip | Bonus action / weapon setup | Adds surface-based weapon damage, often fire from a candle or a prepared coating surface. |
| Help | Action | Picks up a downed adjacent ally when better healing is unavailable; can also remove some adjacent status problems. |
| Non-Lethal Attacks | Toggle | Useful when a fight has quest, moral, or role-play consequences. |
| Ready to Parry | Enemy status | Inspect before spending a major attack into a ready defensive reaction. |
| Action Surge | Fighter feature | Adds burst action economy; strong with [[06_Elixirs_and_Potions#^potion-of-speed\|Potion of Speed]]. |
| Tavern Brawler | Feat / build term | Strong with Throw and high Strength setups. |
| Experimental Alchemy | Transmutation Wizard feature | Can create extra alchemical output; valuable for rare consumables. |
