# 🍷 Elixirs and Potions

Potions and elixirs are not interchangeable. Potions can often be thrown usefully. Elixirs should be drunk directly, because thrown elixirs can last much shorter than expected or fail to work as intended.

Ingredient management is covered in [Alchemy](05_Alchemy.md).

All referenced consumables are summarized in [[15_Appendix#^glossary-consumables|Glossary: Consumables and Alchemy]].

> [!warning] Core habit
> Throw potions when the area effect helps. Drink elixirs directly when you need the full Long-Rest benefit.

## ✅ Decision Guide

**Markers:** ⭐⭐⭐ ⚠️ 🍷

These are the common high-impact choices.

| Consumable | Use when | Avoid when |
|---|---|---|
| [[06_Elixirs_and_Potions#^potion-of-speed\|Potion of Speed]] | The first three rounds can decide the fight, or several allies can be buffed by one thrown potion | The fight may outlast Haste timing and Lethargy would be dangerous |
| [[06_Elixirs_and_Potions#^elixir-of-bloodlust\|Elixir of Bloodlust]] | The character can reliably secure kills, especially against adds | The fight is mostly one durable enemy without kill opportunities |
| [[06_Elixirs_and_Potions#^elixir-of-vigilance\|Elixir of Vigilance]] | Acting before enemies matters more than raw damage | The party already wins initiative reliably |
| [[06_Elixirs_and_Potions#^elixir-of-heroism\|Elixir of Heroism]] | Bless-style reliability is needed all day without normal Bless concentration | Another elixir slot gives more direct value for the character |
| [[06_Elixirs_and_Potions#^elixir-of-cloud-giant-strength\|Cloud Giant Strength]] | A Strength build depends on 27 Strength for damage, carrying, jumps, throws, or shoves | The fight does not reward Strength or the resource is too scarce |
| [[06_Elixirs_and_Potions#^elixir-of-universal-resistance\|Universal Resistance]] | A character must survive repeated heavy damage | The encounter is simple enough for cheaper defensive tools |
| [[06_Elixirs_and_Potions#^remedial-potion\|Remedial Potion]] | Negative conditions could cost turns in a serious fight | It is being used like a normal healing potion |
| [[06_Elixirs_and_Potions#^potion-of-angelic-slumber\|Angelic Slumber]] | One character needs major resources back and can sleep uninterrupted | Combat may start or the sleep may be interrupted |
| [[06_Elixirs_and_Potions#^elixir-of-arcane-cultivation\|Arcane Cultivation]] | A caster needs extra spell slots for a difficult section | Another elixir is more important for survival or initiative |

## ⚡ Potion of Speed ^potion-of-speed

**Markers:** ⭐⭐⭐ ⚔️ 🎯

### ✨ Effect

Grants **Haste** for three rounds. **Lethargy** follows afterward.

### 🧾 Recipe

Main ingredient: [[05_Alchemy#^important-ashes\|Hyena Ear]].

### 🎯 Use

Throw it on the ground before combat if the party is standing close together. One potion can buff multiple party members.

### 🔗 Synergies

Strong with [[06_Elixirs_and_Potions#^elixir-of-bloodlust\|Elixir of Bloodlust]], Fighter, and Action Surge.

### ⚠️ Limitations

The Lethargy after it expires can be dangerous if the fight lasts longer or the timing is poor.

### ✅ Use / Avoid

| Use when | Avoid when |
|---|---|
| The opening rounds decide the encounter, or several grouped allies can share one thrown potion | Lethargy would happen while dangerous enemies are still active |

## 🩸 Elixir of Bloodlust ^elixir-of-bloodlust

**Markers:** ⭐⭐⭐ ⚔️ 🎯

### ✨ Effect

Grants an additional action after a kill.

### 🧾 Recipe

Main ingredient: [[05_Alchemy#^important-ashes\|Warg Fang]].

### 🎯 Use

Especially strong on characters that reliably finish enemies: Fighters, Barbarians, and other high-damage roles.

### 🔗 Synergies

Very strong with [[06_Elixirs_and_Potions#^potion-of-speed\|Potion of Speed]], because more actions create more chances to secure kills.

### ⚠️ Limitations

Weaker against single bosses without adds, because the effect requires kills.

### ✅ Use / Avoid

| Use when | Avoid when |
|---|---|
| The character can reliably finish enemies and convert kills into more actions | There are few or no kill targets |

## ✨ Elixir of Heroism ^elixir-of-heroism

**Markers:** ⭐⭐⭐ ⚔️ ✨

### ✨ Effect

Grants 10 temporary hit points and **Bless** until Long Rest.

### 🧾 Recipe

Main ingredient: [[05_Alchemy#^important-supplements\|Pegasus Feather]].

### 🎯 Use

Very efficient when Bless should stay active without using concentration or spell slots.

### 🔗 Synergies

Strong with a missable Bless-support item from an optional magic area. See [Magic and Useful Items](08_Magic_and_Useful_Items.md).

### ⚠️ Limitations

Uses the elixir slot and therefore competes with [[06_Elixirs_and_Potions#^elixir-of-bloodlust\|Bloodlust]], Giant Strength, and other elixirs.

### ✅ Use / Avoid

| Use when | Avoid when |
|---|---|
| The party wants Bless-style reliability without spending concentration | The character needs Bloodlust, Giant Strength, or a resistance elixir more |

## 💪 Elixir of Hill Giant Strength ^elixir-of-hill-giant-strength

**Markers:** ⭐⭐⭐ ⚔️ ⏱️

### ✨ Effect

Sets Strength to 21 until Long Rest.

### 🧾 Recipe

Main ingredient: [[05_Alchemy#^important-salts\|Hill Giant Fingernails]].

### 🎯 Use

Increases carrying capacity, Strength checks, jump distance, Shove, and Throw. Very good for melee characters and Tavern Brawler builds.

### 🔗 Synergies

Strong with throwing and shoving tactics from [Combat System](02_Combat_System.md).

### ⚠️ Limitations

Uses the elixir slot and competes with offensive elixirs such as [[06_Elixirs_and_Potions#^elixir-of-bloodlust\|Bloodlust]].

### ✅ Use / Avoid

| Use when | Avoid when |
|---|---|
| Strength improves damage, carrying, jump distance, Shove, Throw, or checks | The character does not use Strength meaningfully |

## 🗿 Elixir of Cloud Giant Strength ^elixir-of-cloud-giant-strength

**Markers:** ⭐⭐⭐ ⚠️ ⚔️ 🎯

### ✨ Effect

Sets Strength to 27 until Long Rest.

### 🧾 Recipe

Main ingredient: [[05_Alchemy#^important-salts\|Cloud Giant Fingernails]].

### 🎯 Use

Enables builds with low base Strength. Freed attribute points can be invested into Dexterity and Constitution.

This is the core of elixir-based min-maxing: if a character drinks [[06_Elixirs_and_Potions#^elixir-of-cloud-giant-strength\|Cloud Giant Strength]] every adventuring day, base Strength can be lowered during respec and the saved points can go into Dexterity and Constitution.

### 🔗 Synergies

Especially strong for characters that use many Strength-based attacks, jumps, shoves, or throws.

### ⚠️ Limitations

Blocks the elixir slot for [[06_Elixirs_and_Potions#^elixir-of-bloodlust\|Bloodlust]] or [[06_Elixirs_and_Potions#^elixir-of-heroism\|Heroism]].

It also creates dependency on a rare resource. If [[06_Elixirs_and_Potions#^elixir-of-cloud-giant-strength\|Cloud Giant Strength]] is not available, the build may perform much worse than its character sheet suggests.

[[06_Elixirs_and_Potions#^elixir-of-cloud-giant-strength\|Cloud Giant Strength]] is very rare and is usually acquired through Act 3 vendor inventory luck rather than steady world farming.

### ✅ Use / Avoid

| Use when | Avoid when |
|---|---|
| A build is planned around elixir-based Strength | The resource is scarce and the day does not reward high Strength |

## 👁️ Elixir of Vigilance ^elixir-of-vigilance

**Markers:** ⭐⭐⭐ ⚔️ 🎯

### ✨ Effect

Grants an initiative bonus and prevents being surprised.

### 🧾 Recipe

Main ingredient: [[05_Alchemy#^important-supplements\|Bloodstained Hook]].

### 🎯 Use

Strong for casters, Gloom Stalkers, and parties that want to decide fights with an alpha strike.

### 🔗 Synergies

Especially useful with crowd control, because early turns can remove dangerous enemies from the fight.

### ⚠️ Limitations

Its value drops if initiative is already won reliably.

### ✅ Use / Avoid

| Use when | Avoid when |
|---|---|
| Acting first prevents enemy control, burst, or surprise | Initiative is already consistent without it |

## 🌳 Elixir of Barkskin ^elixir-of-barkskin

**Markers:** ⭐ ⚔️

### ✨ Effect

Sets Armor Class to a fixed value, such as 16 or 17 depending on the exact version.

### 🧾 Recipe

Main ingredient: [[05_Alchemy#^important-salts\|Tree Bark]].

### 🎯 Use

Good for early, poorly armored casters.

### 🔗 Synergies

Can bridge early equipment gaps.

### ⚠️ Limitations

Does not stack cleanly with other Armor Class improvements that already push AC higher. Often becomes unnecessary later once better armor, spells, or equipment bonuses are available.

## 🏛️ Elixir of the Colossus ^elixir-of-the-colossus

**Markers:** ⭐⭐ 🎯

### ✨ Effect

Improves Strength-based play by granting advantage on Strength checks and Strength saving throws.

### 🧾 Recipe

Main ingredient: [[05_Alchemy#^important-salts\|Chasm Creeper]].

### 🎯 Use

Strong for Strength-heavy sequences, environmental puzzles, shoving, throwing, and checks where brute force matters.

### 🔗 Synergies

Works well on characters that already have high Strength or are responsible for Athletics-heavy interactions.

### ⚠️ Limitations

Competes with other elixirs such as [[06_Elixirs_and_Potions#^elixir-of-bloodlust\|Bloodlust]], [[06_Elixirs_and_Potions#^elixir-of-heroism\|Heroism]], and Giant Strength. Use it when Strength checks and saves matter more than raw damage or initiative.

## 🌙 Elixir of Darkvision ^elixir-of-darkvision

**Markers:** ⭐ ⏱️

### ✨ Effect

Grants Darkvision until Long Rest.

### 🧾 Recipe

Main ingredient: [[05_Alchemy#^important-essences\|Crystalline Lens]].

### 🎯 Use

Useful for characters without Darkvision in the Underdark and other dark areas.

### 🔗 Synergies

Helps ranged characters and casters avoid penalties from poor visibility.

### ⚠️ Limitations

Unnecessary for characters that already have reliable Darkvision.

## 🔥 Elixir of Fire Resistance ^elixir-of-fire-resistance

**Markers:** ⭐⭐ ⚔️

### ✨ Effect

Reduces fire damage.

### 🧾 Recipe

Main ingredient: [[05_Alchemy#^important-ashes\|Dragon Egg Mushrooms]].

### 🎯 Use

Standard defensive buff against common fire damage.

Fire is one of the most common damage types. Until [[06_Elixirs_and_Potions#^elixir-of-universal-resistance\|Universal Resistance]] becomes available, [[06_Elixirs_and_Potions#^elixir-of-fire-resistance\|Fire Resistance]] is one of the most useful defensive elixirs.

### 🔗 Synergies

Useful when the party itself works with fire surfaces, explosions, or oil.

### ⚠️ Limitations

Uses the elixir slot and should only be used when fire damage is relevant.

## ☠️ Elixir of Necrotic Resistance ^elixir-of-necrotic-resistance

**Markers:** ⭐ ⚔️

### ✨ Effect

Reduces necrotic damage.

### 🧾 Recipe

Main ingredient: [[05_Alchemy#^important-vitriol\|Onda]].

### 🎯 Use

Important against enemies with heavy necrotic damage.

### 🔗 Synergies

Especially useful on characters that hold enemies in place or take frequent damage.

### ⚠️ Limitations

Situational; provides no benefit against other damage types.

## 🛡️ Elixir of Universal Resistance ^elixir-of-universal-resistance

**Markers:** ⭐⭐⭐ ⚠️ ⚔️

### ✨ Effect

Halves damage from all types until Long Rest.

### 🧾 Recipe

Main ingredient: [[05_Alchemy#^important-vitriol\|Divine Miasma]]. The original recipe line also requires the matching vitriol/sublimate component.

### 🎯 Use

Extremely strong on tanks and characters that take consistent damage in boss fights.

### 🔗 Synergies

Works well with defensive gear and healing resources, because every prevented point of damage saves additional resources.

### ⚠️ Limitations

Very rare resource. Do not waste it on simple fights.

### ✅ Use / Avoid

| Use when | Avoid when |
|---|---|
| A key character expects repeated heavy damage in a hard fight | Cheaper resistance or positioning solves the problem |

## 💚 Potion of Superior Healing ^potion-of-superior-healing

**Markers:** ⭐⭐ ⚔️

### ✨ Effect

Strong healing, roughly in the 15-40 HP range. Use it as the cheaper mid-to-high healing option below [[06_Elixirs_and_Potions#^potion-of-supreme-healing\|Supreme Healing]].

### 🧾 Recipe

Main ingredient: [[05_Alchemy#^important-salts\|Musk Creepers]].

### 🎯 Use

Good healing option when basic healing potions are no longer enough.

### 🔗 Synergies

Can be moved between party members in combat or thrown when appropriate.

### ⚠️ Limitations

Do not waste it on minor damage.

## 💖 Potion of Supreme Healing ^potion-of-supreme-healing

**Markers:** ⭐⭐⭐ ⚔️

### ✨ Effect

Very strong healing, roughly in the 30-60 HP range. Useful for large health swings on tanks and heavily injured characters.

### 🧾 Recipe

Main ingredient: [[05_Alchemy#^important-supplements\|Kirin Hair]].

### 🎯 Use

Save for tanks and heavily injured characters.

### 🔗 Synergies

Strong in long fights where one character must remain combat-ready.

### ⚠️ Limitations

[[05_Alchemy#^important-supplements\|Kirin Hair]] is important and should not be consumed carelessly.

## 🧯 Remedial Potion ^remedial-potion

**Markers:** ⭐⭐⭐ ⚠️ ⚔️

### ✨ Effect

Removes several negative conditions, including problems such as blindness, silence, paralysis, and poison.

### 🧾 Recipe

Main ingredient: [[05_Alchemy#^important-salts\|Zor Scales]].

### 🎯 Use

Every character should ideally carry one in the endgame.

### 🔗 Synergies

Especially important in boss fights with many control or damage conditions.

### ⚠️ Limitations

Do not treat it as a normal healing option; its value is in removing conditions.

### ✅ Use / Avoid

| Use when | Avoid when |
|---|---|
| A condition is likely to cost an important turn | Only HP needs to be restored |

## 😴 Potion of Angelic Slumber ^potion-of-angelic-slumber

**Markers:** ⭐⭐⭐ 🎯 ✨

### ✨ Effect

Creates a Long-Rest effect for one character outside combat after two rounds of uninterrupted sleep.

### 🧾 Recipe

Main ingredient: [[05_Alchemy#^important-essences\|Planetar Feather]].

### 🎯 Use

Use only when uninterrupted sleep is possible. Very strong for restoring the key resources of a single character.

### 🔗 Synergies

Strong with [[06_Elixirs_and_Potions#^elixir-of-arcane-cultivation\|Elixir of Arcane Cultivation]].

### ⚠️ Limitations

Works only outside combat and only when sleep is uninterrupted.

### ✅ Use / Avoid

| Use when | Avoid when |
|---|---|
| One character needs major resource recovery before a difficult section | Enemies, dialogue, or movement may interrupt the sleep |

## 🔮 Elixir of Arcane Cultivation ^elixir-of-arcane-cultivation

**Markers:** ⭐⭐⭐ 🎯 ✨

### ✨ Effect

Grants additional spell slots. The slots are added, not merely restored.

### 🧾 Recipe

Main ingredients: [[05_Alchemy#^important-essences\|Beholder Eyes]] or [[05_Alchemy#^important-suspensions\|Solution of Night Orchid]].

### 🎯 Use

Very strong for casters that need more spell slots before difficult fights.

Higher variants can grant higher-level slots, such as level 3 or level 4 slots, depending on the version of the elixir.

### 🔗 Synergies

Strong synergy with [[06_Elixirs_and_Potions#^potion-of-angelic-slumber\|Potion of Angelic Slumber]], because the added spell slot benefit can be paired with targeted resource recovery.

### ⚠️ Limitations

Uses the elixir slot and competes with defensive or offensive elixirs.

### ✅ Use / Avoid

| Use when | Avoid when |
|---|---|
| Extra spell slots decide the next section | The caster needs initiative, defense, or another elixir more |

## 🧠 Tadpole Elixir ^tadpole-elixir

**Markers:** ⭐ ⚠️ 🎯

### ✨ Effect

Provides a high-risk offensive buff suited to glass-cannon play.

### 🧾 Recipe

Main ingredient: [[05_Alchemy#^important-supplements\|Tongue of Madness]].

### 🎯 Use

Use only when the character benefits from aggressive burst planning and the party can handle the downside.

### 🔗 Synergies

Best considered for characters built to end fights quickly rather than absorb damage.

### ⚠️ Limitations

[[05_Alchemy#^important-supplements\|Tongue of Madness]] should not be spent casually in an Any slot, because it is tied to this specific elixir.

## 🧰 Utility Potions Worth Keeping ^utility-potions

**Markers:** ⭐⭐ ⚠️ ⏱️ 🍷

Some potions are not raw damage tools, but they solve exploration, dialogue, scouting, and emergency problems. Do not sell every utility potion just because it is not part of the main damage plan.

| Consumable | Practical use |
|---|---|
| Potion of Animal Speaking | Saves a spell slot and opens animal interactions until Long Rest |
| Potion of Mind Reading | Gives extra dialogue options until Long Rest when a caster is not prepared for it |
| Potion of Feather Fall | Makes vertical routes and large drops much safer |
| Potion of Invisibility | Scouting, repositioning, escaping, or starting a fight on better terms |
| Potion of Flying | Bypasses difficult terrain and reaches targets or objectives faster |
| Potion of Gaseous Form | Helps reach awkward spaces or avoid normal body-blocking problems |
| Basilisk Oil | Keep at least one when petrification may matter |

Source videos: [Potions Tier List - Cephalopocalypse](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HygsHA55jNU), [Should You Be Using Alchemy in Baldur's Gate 3? - ItalianSpartacus](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lEnF5Xj2dFc), [Baldur's Gate 3: The Ultimate Buff Stacking Guide - SwoleBenji](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fjS7vvau0o0&t=1461s).

## ☠️ Shared Weapon Coatings ^shared-weapon-coatings

**Markers:** ⭐⭐ ⚠️ ⚔️ 🎯 🍷

Some poisons and toxins are better as a shared setup tool than as a single consumed item. Before a hard fight, throw the coating on the ground, enter turn-based mode if timing matters, and have multiple weapon users **Dip** into the surface.

| Use when | Avoid when |
|---|---|
| Several weapon users can benefit from the same coating before combat | The party cannot safely stand near the surface or timing is uncertain |
| The fight is likely to end within the coating duration | The coating would expire before combat starts |
| You want to save multiple copies of the same poison or toxin | The item has a unique effect that only works when consumed directly |

Do not confuse this with elixirs. Important elixirs should still be drunk directly for their full Long-Rest value.

Source: [Baldur's Gate 3: The Ultimate Buff Stacking Guide - SwoleBenji](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fjS7vvau0o0&t=1382s).

## 🎯 Throwing Rules ^throwing-rules

**Markers:** ⭐⭐⭐ ⚠️ 🎯

Potions and elixirs behave differently when thrown.

| Item type | Better use |
|---|---|
| [[06_Elixirs_and_Potions#^potion-of-speed\|Potion of Speed]] | Can be thrown before combat to buff multiple grouped characters |
| Healing potions | Can often be thrown when positioning supports it |
| Giant Strength elixirs | Drink directly; throwing can reduce them to a short duration |
| [[06_Elixirs_and_Potions#^elixir-of-arcane-cultivation\|Arcane Cultivation]] elixirs | Drink directly; important variants may not work properly when thrown |

Important elixirs should be drunk intentionally, not thrown. Throwing an elixir such as [[06_Elixirs_and_Potions#^elixir-of-cloud-giant-strength\|Cloud Giant Strength]] can turn a Long-Rest buff into a short-duration effect, which wastes a rare resource.

For healing potions, aim at the ground near the ally rather than directly at the character when possible. The goal is to splash the healing effect, not to add unnecessary impact damage. Summons or low-priority characters can sometimes deliver a thrown potion when a main character's action is more valuable.

Life Cleric-style healing bonuses can make thrown healing potions much stronger when several allies are grouped. This can turn one potion into meaningful group recovery, but it requires careful aim and party positioning.
