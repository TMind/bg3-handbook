# Camp Characters

Characters currently not in the active party but available to swap in.

## Gale — Evocation Wizard
- **Role**: AoE damage specialist
- **INT**: 17
- **Subclass**: Evocation — AoE spells do NOT hit allies, so he can blast freely on top of Lae'zel
- **Swap in when**: you need more AoE damage output or are facing large groups of enemies
- **Synergy**: Kao (control) + Gale (nuke) is the strongest double-wizard spell combo

## Shadowheart — Trickery Domain Cleric
- **Role**: Support, illusions, stealth utility, secondary healer
- **WIS**: 17
- **Subclass**: Trickery — illusions, Pass Without Trace, sneak support
- **Note**: Does not replace a frontline tank. Running Shadowheart means the party is light on melee defense.
- **Swap in when**: you want a stealth/utility-heavy approach and Stefan is in camp

## Party Composition Notes
- **Current active party**: Kao, Astarion, Lae'zel, Stefan
- Swapping Gale in for Astarion: more AoE, less stealth/trapping
- Swapping Shadowheart in for Lae'zel: more support/utility, significantly weaker frontline
- Lae'zel is considered essential for the current "aura blender" strategy
