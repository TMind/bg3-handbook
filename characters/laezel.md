# Lae'zel — Battle Master Fighter

## Stats (Act 2)
- **Class**: Battle Master Fighter
- **STR**: 19
- **DEX**: 18
- **Role**: Frontline tank + damage engine

## Feats
- **Great Weapon Master (GWM)** — +10 damage, –5 to hit (offset by Advantage from party)
- **Next at level 7**: +1 STR / +1 CON (reaches STR 20, which is the biggest power spike — accuracy with GWM is the weak point)
- **After STR 20**: Sentinel, then Savage Attacker

### Feat Decision Notes
- Sentinel was strongly considered at level 7 — it locks enemies inside Spirit Guardians and generates more OA/GWM hits. Strong synergy with the party.
- Heavy Armor Master was considered — only helps defense, not the current weak point (accuracy).
- Once STR is 20, next priority is Sentinel, then Savage Attacker.

## Battle Master Maneuvers
Good picks for this party:
- **Trip Attack** — Prone = Advantage on melee attacks, enemies stuck in Spirit Guardians
- **Pushing Attack** — positional control, push enemies off ledges or back into aura
- **Disarming Attack** — great vs dangerous weapon users

## Combat Role
- Initiates fights: jump/charge into enemy center, use Trip Attack
- Draws aggro and anchors the frontline so casters are safe
- With Sentinel (when taken): enemies hit by Lae'zel can't move → trapped in Spirit Guardians
- Action Surge for nova turns on priority targets

## Key Mechanics
- **Jump/Leap** bypasses Web/Grease — Lae'zel can reach enemies without walking through control zones
- **Edge engagement**: stand at the edge of Web, don't walk through it
- GWM penalty is offset by: Prone (Trip), Spirit Guardians slow, Kao's control spells

## Notes
- Warding Bond target from Stefan — keep Stefan's HP topped off since he shares damage
- Currently has: Action Surge, Battle Master maneuvers, GWM
